##  WORK DONE  
During this week, our primary focus was on identifying and finalizing a suitable project idea. Our guide provided us with multiple problem statements, and as a team, we carefully reviewed each option.

We discussed different ideas by considering factors such as real-world impact, ease of implementation, and technical requirements. Each member shared their opinions, and we compared the advantages and challenges of each problem.

After finalizing the topic, we created the project repository on GitHub and organized the initial folder structure to begin our work.
